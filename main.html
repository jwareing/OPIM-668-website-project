History.

From the National Museum of the Pacific War to the Pioneer Museum, you can learn about every era of Texan and American history. Enjoy the historical architecture of this German-settled town as you stroll up and down main street.

National Museum of the Pacific War

Dining.

Enjoy classic German cuisine, Tex-Mex, new French bistro fare, and everything in between! The restaurant scene in Fredericksburg is thriving, and new eateries are opening every month. You can even try some local wines!

Auslander restaurant

Shopping.

Clothing, local produce, toys and more are available in many locally-owned shops on Main Street and all over town. Come take home a piece of small-town charm!
